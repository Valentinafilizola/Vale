Hi, I’m Valentina.

I graduated from the University of Washington with a Bachelor of Science in Human-Centered Design and Engineering . I’m particularly interested in mixed reality, human robot interaction, and product design.

I use research, human factors, interaction design, and rapid prototyping to make complex technologies feel intuitive. I enjoy moving between digital interfaces and physical systems, translating technical constraints into experiences people can understand and confidently use.

I approach design with curiosity, continuously learning through research, experimentation, and observing how people interact with technology. I believe technology should make its intentions understandable, behave predictably, and support rather than replace human judgment. My work explores how thoughtful interaction design can preserve people’s autonomy, encourage appropriate trust, and give them meaningful control.

Outside of design, I enjoy playing tennis, hiking, and photography.
